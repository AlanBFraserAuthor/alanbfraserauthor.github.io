About Alan B. Fraser

A direct descendant of John Hart — New Jersey delegate and signer of the Declaration of Independence — Alan brings personal lineage and a lifelong passion for American history to every page. His Sacred Honor Series reconstructs the lives behind the signatures, giving voice to the families history overlooked.
